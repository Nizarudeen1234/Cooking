Indian Chicken Gravey

Indian chicken gravy is easy, quick and very delicious basic curry made with simple ingredients.

Step 1: Ingredients
  • 1. Chicken – 500 grams with bone cut into small pieces
  • 2. Chilli Powder – 2 tbsp
  • 3. Garam Masala Powder – 2 tsp
  • 4. Turmeric Powder – 1 Tsp
  • 5. Ginger Garlic Paste – 1 tbsp
  • 6. Salt (as required)
Step 2: Ginger-Garlic Paste Preparation
  • 1. Add in Garlic and Ginger mix it in a Blender and puree.
  • 2. Keep the Ginger-Garlic paste aside.
  • 3. Begin the Chicken Marination.
Step 3: Marinade
  • 1. Take chicken in a bowl.
  • 2. Add chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala powder.
  • 3. Add salt as per the requirements.
  • 4. Add little ginger garlic paste.
  • 5. Now give this a good mix.
Step 4: Saute the Ingredients
  • 1. Heat the pressure cooker.
  • 2. Add 200ml of Oil into the cooker.
  • 3. Add in Sliced onions, saute till it turns pink.
  • 4. Add in a ginger-garlic paste which was prepared earlier.
  • 5. Mix well.
  • 6. Cook till oil separates.
Step 5: Cook the Chicken
  • 1. Add in spice powders.
  • 2. Mix well.
  • 3. Add the marinade chicken.
  • 4. Mix chicken with the marination ingredients
  • 5. Add 1 cup of water and mix well.
  • 6. Saute for 3 - 5 mins.
Step 6: Let the Whistle Go
  • 1. Cover the lid of the cooker and cook on a low flame
    for about 10 - 15 mins for the chicken to let out moisture.
    **It may vary depending on the age of the chicken or the size of the piece.
  • 2. Pressure cook on a medium heat for 3 - 4 whistles.
  • 3. Now turn off the heat and let the steam go all by itself.
  • 4. Taste the gravy and adjust the salt.
  • 5. When the gravy reaches a desired consistency switch off the stove.
  • 6.Add some coriander leaves. Keep covered until you serve.
Step 7: Serve With Love

Serve chicken curry with rice, jeera rice, ghee rice or chapati.
Also, have a simple raita or onion wedges to go on the side.
